Rome, the capital of Italy, is a treasure trove of history and culture with attractions such as the Colosseum, Roman Forum, Vatican City (including St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel), and the Trevi Fountain.

Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto is renowned for its classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses. Highlights include Fushimi Inari Taisha Shrine, and Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.

New York City, USA

NYC is a global hub of art, culture, food, and fashion. Top attractions include Times Square, Central Park, the Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, and world-class museums like the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

Cape Town, South Africa
